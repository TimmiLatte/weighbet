📥 Log Weigh-In

Enter your date and weight — everything else is calculated automatically

Today's Entry
👋 First weigh-in! This sets your starting weight — all future percentages are calculated from here.

You can log a weigh-in every day. Weekly % uses your last entry of each week. Once submitted, only admins can edit entries.

How It Works

Just enter your date and current weight.

✅ Weigh in as often as you like — once a day
✅ Weekly % uses your last weigh-in of that week
✅ Total % is always vs your starting weight
✅ Skip a week — your data is safe, just no weekly % for that week
⚠️ Miss a whole week in a H2H matchup and your opponent wins by default

Your actual weight is only visible to you and admins.
